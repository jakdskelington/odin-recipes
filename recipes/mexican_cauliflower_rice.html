Mexican Cauliflower Rice

Enjoy a common mexican side dish without all the calories.

You won't even notice the cauliflower!

Ingredients

  • Frozen pack of riced cauliflower
  • 1 small tub of no salt tomato sauce
  • 1-2 tbsp chicken boullin
  • 1 tbsp black pepper
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp onion powder
  • 1-2 tsp smoked paprika

Steps

  1. Heat frozen pack in microwave for 5 minutes
  2. Preheat frying pan or small pot on medium heat
  3. Spray pan or pot with notstick cooking spray and add the riced cauliflower
  4. Add 1/2 of tomato sauce (or more if desired)
  5. Add seasoning
  6. Mix until it's all incorporated. Your riced cauliflower should take on that familiar mexican rice color
  7. Taste for seasoning and add more if needed. Otherwise, once it's all incorporated you're done!
  8. Garnished top with cilantro or chopped chives (optional). Enjoy!
